# Design Templates - Quick Start Guide

## What Are Design Templates?

Design Templates allow you to quickly change your dealership website's look and feel by applying pre-configured color schemes and fonts with a single click.

## How to Use Design Templates

### Step 1: Access Settings
1. Log in to your admin panel
2. **For Admin users:** Select a dealership from the dropdown (top left)
3. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar

### Step 2: Browse Templates
At the top of the Settings page, you'll see the **Design Templates** section with two categories:

**Pre-set Templates** (8 professional designs):
- Modern Blue
- Classic Black
- Luxury Gold
- Sporty Red
- Elegant Silver
- Eco Green
- Premium Navy
- Sunset Orange

**Your Custom Templates** (templates you've created)

### Step 3: Apply a Template
1. Click **"Apply Template"** on any template card
2. The colors and font will automatically update in the form below
3. Scroll down and click **"Save Changes"** to apply the template to your website

### Step 4: Save Your Own Templates

Once you've customized your colors and font to your liking:

1. Click **"Save Current as Template"** (top right)
2. Enter a name for your template (e.g., "Summer Sale Theme")
3. Add an optional description
4. Click **"Save Template"**

Your custom template will now appear in the "Your Custom Templates" section and can be reapplied anytime!

### Step 5: Manage Custom Templates

**To delete a custom template:**
1. Find it in "Your Custom Templates"
2. Click the red **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm the deletion

Note: Pre-set templates cannot be deleted.

## Template Preview

Each template card shows:
- **Name and description** - What the template is called
- **Three color squares** - Primary, Secondary, and Background colors
- **Font name** - The font that will be used

## Tips

✅ **Try Before You Save**: Apply templates to see how they look before saving changes  
✅ **Create Multiple Templates**: Save templates for different seasons or promotions  
✅ **Use Descriptive Names**: Name templates clearly (e.g., "Holiday Red & Green")  
✅ **Preview First**: Check the color previews in the settings form after applying
